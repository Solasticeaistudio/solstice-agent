# War Room First Wave Campaign

## Purpose

This is the first outbound motion for War Room using Solstice Agent's outreach subsystem. The priority is not volume. The priority is fast learning, clean positioning, and booked conversations with plausible buyers.

## Positioning

Lead with:

- strategic decision stress-testing
- board-ready decision brief in hours
- paid Decision Sprint
- counterfactual and vulnerability analysis

Do not lead with:

- generic AI consulting
- engine counts
- broad platform claims
- investor-relations language

## Contact Strategy

- For named operators or portfolio leads, send a direct personalized initial email.
- For investor-relations inboxes, optimize for internal routing rather than a hard sell.
- For large enterprises, use concise copy that can be forwarded internally without editing.
- Keep every first touch draft-first until the campaign language is validated.

## Qualification Rules

Advance a lead if:

- they ask for sample outputs
- they ask who internally should own it
- they route you to strategy, corp dev, portfolio ops, or innovation
- they reference an active decision or pilot candidate

Hold or deprioritize a lead if:

- they reply with generic vendor process only
- they only want collateral with no context
- they route to procurement without an owner

Mark lost if:

- they explicitly decline
- they ask not to be contacted
- the organization is clearly outside the War Room use case
