# War Room Personas And Segments

## Segment Priority

### Wave 1

1. Private equity and portfolio operations
2. Risk, data, and analytics platforms
3. Insurance and reinsurance strategy buyers
4. Defense and national security platforms

### Why This Order

- PE and portfolio ops can buy quickly and understand paid diagnostic work.
- Risk and analytics firms can buy directly or partner.
- Insurance buyers fit well but require tighter risk-language.
- Defense can be high value but slower and more bureaucratic.

## Buyer Personas

### Private Equity / Portfolio Ops

Who they are:

- operating partners
- portfolio operations leaders
- value creation leads
- corporate development leaders

What they care about:

- pre-deal diligence
- fragile investment theses
- post-acquisition execution risk
- portfolio triage
- board communication

Message:

- stress-test an active deal, portfolio thesis, or turnaround plan
- compress diligence and expose hidden failure points

CTA:

- offer a paid sprint on one live deal or one portfolio decision

### Risk / Data / Analytics

Who they are:

- strategy leaders
- product leaders
- analytics executives
- advisory leaders

What they care about:

- differentiated decision products
- executive-grade outputs
- scenario speed
- client-facing value

Message:

- War Room can act as a strategic decision-intelligence module or a pilot for client-facing scenario work

CTA:

- offer a small pilot around one active customer or internal strategic decision

### Insurance / Reinsurance

Who they are:

- risk strategy
- corporate development
- innovation
- portfolio management

What they care about:

- tail-risk triggers
- mitigation paths
- exposure surfaces
- speed to strategic clarity

Message:

- stress-test catastrophe, portfolio, or reinsurance structure decisions

CTA:

- offer a paid sprint on a live portfolio, hedging, or market move

### Defense / NatSec

Who they are:

- strategy
- mission engineering
- innovation
- program risk leaders

What they care about:

- mission posture
- contested assumptions
- critical decision support
- board and leadership briefings

Message:

- use War Room to pressure-test mission decisions, program paths, or acquisition assumptions

CTA:

- ask for the right owner in strategy, innovation, or mission engineering

## Routing Guidance

If the initial contact is not the buyer, ask for routing to one of:

- Corporate Strategy
- Portfolio Operations
- Value Creation
- Corporate Development
- Risk Strategy
- Innovation
- Mission Engineering

Avoid asking to be routed to:

- generic sales
- investor relations as the final destination
- recruiting
- customer support
